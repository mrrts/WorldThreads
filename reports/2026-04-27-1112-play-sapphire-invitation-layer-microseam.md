## The lesson learned

Once the trust layer is honestly inspectable, the reader stops fighting your
claims and starts noticing your posture.

That is what happened here.

The remaining friction is no longer about whether the page is telling the truth
about the product. It is about whether the page is slightly **telling the reader
how to feel** or slightly **narrating its own significance**.

That gives the governing lesson:

**after trust is earned, invitation lines should suggest rather than declare.**

Or even tighter:

**let the crown invite; don't tell the reader what longing it proves they have.**
