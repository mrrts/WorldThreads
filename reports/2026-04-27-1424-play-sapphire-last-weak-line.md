## The actual finding

The sapphire page now has **one clearly weakest line**.

It is:

`You are seeking wonder sturdy enough to survive daylight and still feel human in the hand.`

That does not mean it is terrible. It means it is now the last remaining line
that multiple readers independently experience as the page speaking for them a
little too quickly.

The strongest line-family on the page is now also clear:

- `Read the doctrine and prompt stack in the open.`
- `If this page catches your eye, the README is the next honest surface.`

Those lines are the opposite shape:

- they do not narrate the user's interior
- they point outward toward verification
- they feel confident without needing aura

## The lesson learned

The page has reached a new stage of health.

Earlier, the problem was:

- `prove the page is not overclaiming`

Then it became:

- `trim the remaining invitation-layer perfume`

Now it is even simpler:

- `stop telling the reader what longing they have`

That is the whole live lesson from this pass.

If the crown is going to work, it works best when it invites wonder without
announcing the seeker's heart on their behalf.
