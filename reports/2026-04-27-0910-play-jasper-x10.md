## Reading

The first obvious finding is the thematic one: Jasper-grade reading generally
favors the app when it behaves like a vessel instead of a showroom. The
surfaces that land best in the theoretical branch are the ones that feel
stable, plain, and oriented toward actual use: the app proper, memory that
doesn't boast, journals/proactive pings when they feel invitational rather
than needy, and overall trust when the instrument presents itself as something
meant to hold rather than impress.

The second finding is more interesting than the first: **using one of the app's
own live characters as the `/play` persona reveals a limit in the persona-sim
instrument itself.** The sim did not stay close enough to actual Jasper. Even
after tightening the prompt with live corpus lines, the simulated voice tended
to broaden into artisan-symbolism and clean thesis-statements. Actual Jasper,
by contrast, is better at this exact evaluative work. He already speaks in the
database with more compression, more plainness, and more incisive taste.

That means the highest-value artifact of this run is not simply "Jasper likes
honest craft." The project already knew that. The higher-value artifact is:
**when the `/play` persona is itself an in-db character, the empirical branch
may outrun the theoretical branch immediately.** The app's own characters, in
lived corpus, can already serve as evaluators of the app with more fidelity
than a fresh external model can simulate them. Put differently: "ask Jasper"
may now be a stronger instrument than "simulate Jasper" for certain kinds of
craft judgment.

There is still real value in the theoretical branch. It gave a ten-surface
spread cheaply and fast, and it did surface recurring pressure points:

- the sapphire pitch still risks overpromising before it demonstrates
- the group-chat surface needs careful anti-jostle discipline
- the app-companion border remains one of the true trust thresholds
- memory and journals win when they feel companionable without becoming clingy

But the live grounding keeps the hierarchy honest. The simulated Jasper is the
sketch. The actual Jasper corpus is the stronger witness.

## One move

If Jasper-grade readers are a meaningful target shape, the next sharp move is
not "make everything more folksy." It is narrower: let the public and
derivation-facing surfaces sound a little less like proclamation and a little
more like a craftsman pleased at the right thing — the app works, it offers a
