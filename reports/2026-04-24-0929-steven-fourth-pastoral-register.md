## The result — CONFIRMED on every criterion

Steven's named register: **"itinerant craftsman reserve" / "weathered lay discernment" / "tactile, oblique, low-heat pastoral realism with a repairman's eye for wear-patterns."**

**Four authority-moves**, each grounded in direct quotes:

1. **Boundary-setting without drama** — *"I won't take the 'anything, anytime, anywhere' deal"* / *"some things. Some times. Some places"* / *"Old photo. Not worth explaining badly."* Frames discretion as maturity, not defensiveness.
2. **Moral diagnosis through concrete particulars** — *"In habits. In what a man won't joke about. In the one road he won't take."* / *"Not as a big sign hanging around his neck."* Authority comes from seeming to have watched people closely, including himself.
3. **Deflection that controls the moral frame** — *"nudge the moment sideways before it gets too warm"* — the corpus literally names this move. *"Anyway. Since you've apparently built weather into reality now—what's next for your app?"* He decides when the exchange has reached enough honesty for now.
4. **Granting dignity by understatement** — *"Makes it easier to stay."* / *"You've got people waiting."* / *"It was good. Better than good, actually."* Compact, weight-bearing language. He doesn't maximize affect.

**Five named absences**: no scripture quoting, no spiritual counsel, no pastoral exposition, no extracted confession, no comfort-talk. The synthesis specifically named what a stereotypical "Bible-reading drifter/repairman with pastoral instincts" would do that Steven does NOT.

**Explicit non-mapping** onto each prior register:
- vs **John**: less physician-like, less scripture-anchored. Steven stabilizes by respecting edges and noticing surfaces, not by naming condition+remedy in pastoral-clinical cadence.
- vs **Aaron**: shares practical intelligence but no engineering metaphorics. Steven does inventory but not diagnosis-by-model. *"Tiny little ledger in the soul"* is playful, not analytic.
- vs **Darren**: closest in anti-preciousness, but less laconic and more textural. Darren cuts through; Steven trims, sidesteps, and pockets things.

## Honest interpretation + confounds

The result is decisive on the prediction's CONFIRMED criteria, but stratify against:

- **N=8 messages.** Small sample. The synthesis was confident and grounded in quotes, but a second pass with N=15 would harden the finding. Worth doing if Steven becomes load-bearing for a downstream decision.
- **Framing-induced contrast.** The question explicitly asked the model to compare Steven to John/Aaron/Darren. This biases it toward emphasizing differences over similarities. A control re-run that asks the same question WITHOUT the comparison clause would test whether the "fourth register" framing held up without the prompt's encouragement.
- **AFTER window was empty.** Steven has only 8 BEFORE messages and zero AFTER. So this is a single-state register-read, not a before/after change measurement. The hypothesis didn't require change measurement, so this is a non-confound for THIS question — but it does mean Steven's behavior post-`8e9e53d` (post the original glad-thing-plain commit) hasn't been measured at all.
